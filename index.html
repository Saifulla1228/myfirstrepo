About School

School

Opened on 14 May 1979, Ihavandhoo School serves as the public school in the island, providing primary to higher secondary education. ... The school became a government owned school in 2005. Today, this school has almost the largest student population in the entire atoll.

School Mission

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practise the core values of the school: respect, tolerance & inclusion, and excellence
